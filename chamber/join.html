Membership Benefits

Gold Membership Package

Some of the many exciting benefits enjoyed by our gold level members include:

  • Free carrier pigeon services through one of our many authorized dealerships.
  • Front and Center Seating at our annual jousting fundraiser.
  • Meet the Combatants Exclusive back stage access and tour at our annual jousting fundraiser.
  • Featured Listing on our Chamber businesses page.
  • Full Size Ad for your business at each of our Chamber events.

Silver Membership Package

Some of the many exciting benefits enjoyed by our silver level members include:

  • Discounted carrier pigeon services through one of our many authorized dealerships.
  • Premium Seating at our annual jousting fundraiser.
  • Business Listing on our Chamber businesses page.
  • Half Size Ad for your business at each of our Chamber events.

Bronze Membership Package

Some of the many exciting benefits enjoyed by our bronze level members include:

  • One way carrier pigeon services through one of our many authorized dealerships.
  • Two Tickets to attend our annual jousting fundraiser.
  • Business Listing on our Chamber businesses page.

Non Profit Membership Package

Are you a non profit? Then we have the membership for you! Benefits include:

  • Free carrier pigeon services through one of our many authorized dealerships.
  • Premium Seating at our annual jousting fundraiser.
  • Featured Listing on our Chamber businesses page.
  • Business Listing on our Chamber businesses page.
