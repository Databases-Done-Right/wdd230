Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Brett Jameson

WDD 230

Site Name

Camelot Chamber of Commerce. This name represents the city of Camelot in which the chamber of commerce operates.

Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to educate users as to what exactly lazy loading is. It is intended to list some of the benefits of utilizing lazy loading techniques for their website as well as give some basic instruction as to how they can go about implementing it.

Scenarios

  • What events will the chamber of commerce of holding this month?
  • What are some of the local events that are being held that the community might have interest in?
  • What are some of the local businesses that a visitor to the city might be interested in?
  • Where can I find information about the Round Table (Chamber of Commerce) board of directors?
